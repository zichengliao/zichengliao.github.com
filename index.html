Zicheng Liao

Ph.D.

Email:  zliao (AT) zju.edu.cn

I just started as a Lecturer at the Colledge of Computer Science of Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China, where I spent my most wonderful 4 years of college life. The city is featured by the poetic West Lake, the Dragon Well green tea plantation, and the LiuHe Pagoda which brings harmony to the land at the intersection of Qiantang River and mountains.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the computer science department, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) in 2014 with the advice of David Forsyth.
